GAME RULES


  1. The game has two players, playing in rounds
  2. In each turn, the player rolls the dices as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his CURRENT SCORE
  3. But if the player rolls 1 in any of the dices, his CURRENT SCORE gets lost. Morever, if the player rolls 6 in both of the dices, all his GLOBAL SCORES gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  4. The player can choose hold which means that his CURRENT SCORE gets added to his GLOBAL SCORE
  5. The first player to reach 100 (or the score defined by the players) wins the game!
